What is FlatRateVoice?

The Agency Problem

GHL per-minute billing is a client relationship problem.

GHL AI Employee costs $97 per location per month, then adds per-minute voice charges (roughly $0.16 a minute all-in) billed against a prepaid wallet. Your clients get a different invoice every month. You spend time explaining it instead of delivering value.

⚠ GHL AI Employee
  • ●$97/location/month platform fee
  • ●Per-minute voice charges on every call (~$0.16/min all-in)
  • ●Wallet top-ups required to keep calls flowing
  • ●Bill varies every month based on call volume
  • ●You reconcile the difference. Clients ask questions.
✓ FlatRateVoice
  • ●Flat monthly rate — one number per tier
  • ●Pooled minute bank shared across all your locations
  • ●Fixed overage rate shown upfront — no wallet, no guessing
  • ●You know your maximum cost before the month starts
  • ●One number to quote, mark up, and stand behind
ARCHITECTURE

One hop, not three.

Most AI voice products chain transcription, an LLM, then text-to-speech — three layers of latency and cost. FlatRateVoice is built on Gemini 3.1 Flash Live, a native speech-to-speech model. The caller speaks and the agent responds. That is the whole chain.

The demo above is not cherry-picked. It is what every call sounds like. When you present this to a client, you are presenting something audibly better than what they have experienced before. That is a sales tool, not just a feature.

Deployment

From reseller approval to live agent.

Configure once. Deploy to client locations without touching code.

STEP 01

Reseller onboarding

Connect your GHL account and configure white-label settings. Your agency name and branding across all client-facing surfaces.

STEP 02

Client location setup

Configure the agent per client: vertical, hours, qualification logic, calendar, and CRM pipeline. Most configurations take under an hour.

STEP 03

Phone number + A2P

Clients with A2P-approved numbers go live within 24 hours of setup. Without one, A2P registration must clear first — usually a few days, and up to about two weeks depending on carrier vetting.

A2P REGISTRATION

Required for outbound SMS. We handle the submission. Timeline is carrier-controlled — usually a few days. We give you accurate status before you commit to a go-live date with your client.

STEP 04

Agent goes live

Calls forward to the agent. Every lead captured, every appointment booked, every CRM record updated. Monitor from the agency dashboard.

HOW POOLING WORKS

Locations share the bank

Minutes are not assigned per location. The pool covers your entire agency. A location with 10 calls and one with 400 draw from the same bank.

OVERAGE

Fixed rate, billed once

If the pool runs out, overage applies at the fixed rate for your tier. The rate does not change based on how far you go over. You always know what a minute costs.

SCALING UP

Grows with your book

If you consistently hit overage, we flag it before the next billing cycle. Move up a tier: overage cost drops, pool doubles or more.
